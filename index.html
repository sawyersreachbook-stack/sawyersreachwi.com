What Town Hall Can Help You With

Planning a Project? (Building & Land Use)

If you’re thinking about starting a building project, need a Permit, or have questions about how we use our land (Zoning), give our Zoning Officer and Building Administrator, Ellen, a shout. She can help!

*Note: You can get forms photocopied right here at Town Hall during business hours.*

Trash Day Info & Recycling

Bill will be by with his dump truck to pick up garbage when he can. Please make sure you're there to help him if it's something big.

What's Happening Around Town

Regular Community Events

  • Weekly: Bingo Night at the Community Center - Every Thursday evening at 7:00 PM. All ages welcome!
  • Weekly: Friday Night Fish Fry at the Grand Reach Lodge. Starts at 5:00 PM. Always a local favorite!
  • Monthly: Town Hall Community Meeting - Held the first Monday of every month at 7:00 PM. We encourage everyone to come and share a dish!

Important Town Notices

Heads Up, Folks!

  • Winter Parking: When the snow flies, please don't park your vehicles on the street overnight. This helps our plow drivers clear the roads safely and efficiently!
  • Pier Cleanup: We've noticed a bit of a mess down at the local pier. Please remember to clean up all your fish guts and bait scraps. Let's keep our beautiful Reach Lake clean for everyone!
  • A Friendly Reminder to Gary: Gary, old friend, we've had a few calls. Please stop putting gasoline in your sprinkler system. It's not good for the lawn, or the environment, and definitely not safe.
  • Job Seekers: Looking for work? The PowerCore jobs app is a great resource for local openings. Check it out!
