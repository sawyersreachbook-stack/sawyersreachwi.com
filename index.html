Waste & Recycling Collection by Quadrant

Residential Collection Schedule

Your collection day is determined by your quadrant in relation to **Lake Sawyer**. Garbage is collected weekly; Recycling is collected bi-weekly on **A-Week** or **B-Week** schedules.

Zone 1: Northeast Quadrant (NE)

Garbage: Tuesdays

Recycling: A-Week (Tuesdays)

Zone 2: Northwest Quadrant (NW)

Garbage: Wednesdays

Recycling: B-Week (Wednesdays)

Zone 3: Southeast Quadrant (SE)

Garbage: Thursdays

Recycling: A-Week (Thursdays)

Zone 4: Southwest Quadrant (SW)

Garbage: Fridays

Recycling: B-Week (Fridays)

**Next A-Week starts: October 28th.**

Disposal Reminders

  • Electronics (e-waste) must be brought to the Town Transfer Station on the third Saturday of the month.
  • No plastic bags in the recycling bin. Use clear or blue bags only for paper and containers.
  • Yard waste is collected separately during the Spring/Fall cleanup events only.
  • Bulk items (appliance-s, furniture) require a pre-paid sticker. Call Town Hall for pickup.
